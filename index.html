Kosa’s Rescue and Recovery

Kosa was a beautiful blind rescued cat. The first day we found her, she was as thin as a stick. Her situation was really bad, and we did our best to make her healthy again. We found her at the end of 2019, just before the pandemic and lockdown began. She became a huge part of our lives and helped pull me out of sadness, likely protecting me from depression.

Kosa’s Joyful Kittens

After a year with us, Kosa gave birth to three kittens: Fluffy, Saboaa, and a third kitten, who tragically died when Kosa accidentally lay on her. Later, Kosa had another baby, Panda. We had initially thought Kosa’s blindness was due to some cruel act, but when Panda lost one eye due to a disease he inherited from his mother, we realized it was genetic.

The Heartbreak of Loss

Kosa and her kittens lived in a space in front of our house where they could enjoy the freedom of playing in a large area like wild cats. Sadly, one day they all became sick and died shortly after. The devastation I felt was indescribable. I only hope that whoever caused their deaths faces a terrible fate.
